Living with a cat is fun.<\/h2>\n

There are a lot of perks to sharing a house with a furry feline friend. You will have a sleepover with your best friend every single day. They will always be there to listen to all your problems. And even though they can\u2019t really offer any solutions to those problems, they can always cuddle with you until you feel better. And their warm and cosy cuddles can really heal us. You will neer feel alone with your furry friend around. They will always keep you company. Every now and then they will make you laugh with their hilarious antics as well. Life with cats is just pure entertainment. However, life with cats is not always sunshine and rainbows. If you have ever lived with a cat, you know that sometimes it can be complete chaos.<\/p>\n

How is it chaos? Wait till you\u2019re trying to sleep at 3 AM and you hear things break all around your room. Or having your cat follow you literally everywhere you go. They will be your shadow!
