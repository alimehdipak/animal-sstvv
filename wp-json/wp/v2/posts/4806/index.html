This board decides who which cat was the naughtiest and which was the nicest.<\/h2>\n

As Christmas is approaching, everyone is sort of anxious as to whether their name will be on Santa Claus\u2019 naughty list or nice list. We all kind of want to be on the nice list, that\u2019s the ideal situation. Nobody wants to be spanked by Santa unless they are on the nice list and that\u2019s the gift they want. Sorry for my dark humor if it offended anyone.<\/p>\n

Swiftly moving on, we know that Santa Claus creates these lists for humans but what about animals? Specifically, when it comes to distinguishing animals between a naughty list and a nice list, you can\u2019t help but think of cats. They have very diverse attitudes and behavior. Thankfully, we have\u00a0Heaven on Earth<\/a>, a rescue company based in Los Angeles that devotes itself to rescuing cats. They are basically the Santa Claus for cats. They have a dedicated \u201cnaughty and nice\u201d board where they put up pictures of cats that have either been super naughty or super nice, out of the ones under their care.<\/p>\n

Today we are going to look at their board and see which cats made it. Whether naughty or nice, they all look super cute.<\/p>\n
