    Paul Robertson is a proud truck driver. It\u2019s undeniable that he loves his job so much, in fact, he\u2019s been doing it for years now.However, whenever he\u2019s trailing on a long road, lonesome often gets in his way.Let\u2019s admit it, staying awake while driving alone at night isn\u2019t easy, but there\u2019s no stopping Paul especially if he has to hit a certain deadline.<\/p>\n

    In his back and forth trips, he had a feline companion named Howie.They shared countless hours on the road and were best buddies for years.     He was utterly heartbroken when he lost his beloved feline.<\/p>\n

    But that didn\u2019t stop him from wanting another animal companion. He soon visited an animal shelter and looked for another feline. There, he was introduced to a ginger cat named Percy.<\/p>\n
