     <\/p>\n

    Toro is a five-year-old pup with a painful past.<\/p>\n

    Toro, alongside two other pups, was rescued and brought to a shelter after he was found chained to a fence outside an abandoned house.<\/p>\n

    The poor pup was weighed down by the nearly 16-pound-heavy chain and it is believed that he\u2019s spent his entire life outside.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    After Toro and his co-prisoners were unchained and removed from the abandoned house, they were brought to the\u00a0Town of Hempstead Animal Shelter<\/a>\u00a0where they received the attention and care they needed.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

     <\/p>\n

    Toro was initially very shy and scared and didn\u2019t necessarily seem to understand that he was finally safe.<\/p>\n

    It looked like all those years of neglect had perhaps broken the little pup\u2019s spirit.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    After having been so betrayed and mistreated by humans in the past, it would have been understandable if Toro never trusted another human being ever again.<\/p>\n

    But as it turns out, that\u2019s not how Toro intends to live his life.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Slowly, Toro began warming up and trusting his new surroundings more and more, and it became clear that the little pup has a big and affectionate heart.<\/p>\n

    Though it takes him a while to get comfortable around new people, it is more than obvious that the little guy just wants to love and be loved.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    One day, after having grown successfully more comfortable around the people at the shelter, Toro decided to show his love for his rescuers in the most adorable way.<\/p>\n

    Toro was out in the yard playing around when he suddenly approached staff member Melissa Fogarty.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    To her surprise, the pup snuggled up in her lap and pressed himself against her chest in a heartfelt hug.<\/p>\n

    This turned out of to be the first hug of many, as Toro soon began hugging everyone he considered a friend.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Now, Toro is known around the shelter for his sweet hugs and everyone loves him dearly.<\/p>\n

    Toro can still get a little bit shy and nervous and isn\u2019t always super comfortable with the hustle and bustle that living at a shelter sometimes entails, but he loves and trusts everyone working there<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Everyone is rooting for sweet little Toro to find his perfect forever home, but he\u2019s already spent a year at the shelter without finding a family that\u2019s ready to give him the love and patience he needs.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Toro briefly moved in with a potential family this last January but was returned to the shelter after less than two weeks.<\/p>\n

    Due to his past trauma, the sweet boy needs a family that\u2019s ready to provide him with a lot of love, structure, training and patience.<\/p>\n

     <\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    For the right family, Toro is sure to bring so much love, joy and hugs into their lives.<\/p>\n

    He\u2019s an incredibly affectionate pup and truly deserves a warm and loving home.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

     <\/p>\n

    Everyone is surprised that it has taken Toro so long to find a family, but now that he\u2019s gained some exposure online, he\u2019ll hopefully soon be spotted by his future forever family.<\/p>\n
