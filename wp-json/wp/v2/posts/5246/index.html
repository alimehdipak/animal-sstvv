The mother horse died shortly after this deformed foal was born, although it was previously healthy.<\/p>\n

Recently, the birth of a young horse with a mixed monstrous shape has made the people of Zarand town, Arad county, Romania bewildered because they fear that this is a mature animal. can take anyone\u2019s life.
\n
\nA pony with an unusually large humped head and deformed legs was born at around 1:00 a.m. on Saturday, April 5. It is known that this mutation phenomenon is the result of an inborn genetic disorder during pregnancy. Due to the lack of timely caesarean section, the mother horse died a few minutes after giving birth.
\n
\n
\nAccording to experts, this congenital genetic disorder in the foal is caused by a genetic mutation or a genital disease that the mother horse has. The disease caused the foal\u2019s skull to grow larger than normal to accommodate the overflowing brain fluid.<\/p>\n

Based on the shape of the deformed skull as well as the protrusion of the eyes and the shorter upper jaw than the lower jaw, doctors believe that this young horse has hydrocephalus, also known as water brain.<\/p>\n
