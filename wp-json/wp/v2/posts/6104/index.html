    Dogs\u2019 owners are everything they have for the rest of their lives. They admired each other and formed a deep bond throughout the relationship. Yet, for whatever reason, some owners abandon their dogs in the middle of nowhere. Dogs are one of the most loyal creatures on the planet. They wait innocently for their owners to return, not knowing that they have been abandoned forever.<\/p>\n

     <\/p>\n

    Since 2001, Kritter Klub has been recording animal stories with a group of Korean animal lovers.\u00a0<\/span>They\u00a0<\/span>were notified<\/span>\u00a0one day about a homeless dog in a village who constantly rushing\u00a0<\/span>toward<\/span>\u00a0cars.<\/span>\u00a0Nobody knew why he was acting strangely. A villager claimed that every time he returned home from work, this dog would follow him and sit next to his car. The<\/span>\u00a0peasant was perplexed since he had no idea who the dog was or where he came from.<\/span><\/p>\n

    The dog has been there for more than 20 days, and has not left his place along the way, as if looking for something, he would miss it when he moved. Whenever a white car passed by, he immediately chased it far away. Then he returned to his normal position and continued to wait regretfully. What was the lonely dog looking forward to?<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    It wasn\u2019t until the team met a neighbor who had been watching the dog for a month that they discovered his backstory. A white automobile arrived here a month ago, then left the dog alone on an unknown street. From then on, he began to pursue any white car in the hopes of locating his owner. The old guy attempted several times to help him and took him home, but the dog refused and fled away. He still longed for his master and waited for him no matter what time of day or night it was, even when the weather grew colder and colder. However, the person never showed up.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

     <\/p>\n

    Fortunately, the person who alerted the team decided to accept the sad puppy as a member of his family. The dog is still very sad, but he is gradually adjusting to the new life. He was given a comfortable abode that the guy had prepared for him, as well as the name Baduk. He was waiting on the road again, and this time there was a kind person beside him.<\/p>\n

     <\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Witnessing the dog struggle to survive in the bitter weather was upsetting, so members of the Kritter Klub took the ailing puppy to the clinic for a check-up. He was in a good condition, but he was depressed since he couldn\u2019t believe that his owner had betrayed him.<\/p>\n
