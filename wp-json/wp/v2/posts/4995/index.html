    Pikachu is an adorable and incredibly famous character from the popular franchise Pok\u00e9mon, in which adorable little creatures called Pok\u00e9mon (derived from the name \u201cPocket monsters\u201d) battle each other and take part in different adventures.<\/p>\n

    Pok\u00e9mon are very cute, and they are often very interestingly designed. There\u2019s no doubt that many of us have, at one time or another in our lives, imagined what it would be like if Pok\u00e9mon were real, and didn\u2019t just exist as cute animations.<\/p>\n

    Well, if you\u2019ve ever wanted to see a Pok\u00e9mon in real life, you\u2019re in luck\u2026 sort of.<\/p>\n

     <\/p>\n

    \n
    \n
    <\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n

     <\/p>\n

    While not a real Pok\u00e9mon, this beautiful little girl\u2019s unusual colouring has left the internet in awe, and people have excitedly compared her to the famous Pickachu.<\/p>\n

    Hoewer, she is, in fact, a golden brushtail possum!<\/p>\n

    The little possum was rescued after having fallen off her mother\u2019s back and been left vulnerable and alone.<\/p>\n

    Luckily, a kind soul found and rescued her, and brought her to the\u00a0Boronia Veterinary Clinic and Hospital\u00a0in VIC, Australia.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Apparently, golden brushtail possums are simply common brushtail possums \u2013 just with yellow fur instead of brown.<\/p>\n

    The golden colour comes from a particular mutation, which causes low levels of melanin.<\/p>\n

    And as it turns out, although the most common colour for brushtail possums is brown, they can also be black, silver\/grey, red, or yellow.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Unfortunately, the golden possums\u2019 bright fur makes them stand out to predators, which is one reason for why they are so rare.<\/p>\n

    This lucky little girl, however, did not have to worry about all that. She got all the care, comfort and safety she needed after being rescued, and went on to live with a carer until it was time to fo her to be released into a wildlife preserve.<\/p>\n

    The golden possum\u2019s unique beauty has delighted many people and has put smiles on countless faces, including ours.<\/h3>\n
