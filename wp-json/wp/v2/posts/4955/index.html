    Mitchell Miner, 15, and his cow Audri participated in the Lowe State Cauldron Fair.
    \nUnfortunately, they didn\u2019t manage to win a notable place, but that didn\u2019t discourage them. This day was so exhausting that the boy fell asleep, with his favourite cow Audrey! This caring cow did something that is not very typical for an animal. She hugged the tired boy as if to comfort him for a bad result. They were fifth out of a total of seven participants. When the boy\u2019s father entered the barn to look for his son, he found this touching scene.<\/p>\n

    He looked sad, but also full of love. He immediately took a camera to capture the moment.
    \nWhen he posted the video online, the comments were more than good, and the father wrote:
    \n\u201cOur son Mitchell and his heifer after yesterday\u2019s show\u201d. The video immediately went viral and received more than 15,000 likes by the next day.
    \nThe boy\u2019s parents were thrilled by this unusual connection between a man and a cow. They have always tried to teach their children about life in the countryside. They believed that a good person consists of love and care for animals.<\/p>\n
