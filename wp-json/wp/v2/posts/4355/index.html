    Beaux Tox is a Labrador Retriever unique in his look. He has a wrinkled forehead that makes him look like he\u2019s perpetually concerned about something, and he was neglected by many just because of this flaw. His condition was caused by being squashed in his mother\u2019s womb while sharing space with five other siblings. Apart from this peculiar characteristic, Beaux is just like any other dog, and his head deformity didn\u2019t affect his intelligence or life expectancy, as an MRI confirmed. He definitely has so much to offer, if only people gave him a chance.
    \nWhen he was a puppy his breeders gave him away for free, because they weren\u2019t able to sell him, and the man who adopted him confined Beaux in the backyard after he found out the dog didn\u2019t get along with his cats. During the 5 years with that man, who left him in a very malnourished state. During that time, the man tried to get rid of Beaux, and local rescue groups tried to find him a forever home, but he was given up again and again, always being returned to the man\u2019s backyard.
    \nThankfully, things didn\u2019t stay that way for Beaux because Jamie Hulit was sent from above to give Beaux another chance. Jamie knew what it was like to be in foster care as she once went through it when she was 9 years old. That\u2019s why she decided to adopt Beaux, and with the help of Dr. Jay Rydberg, Beaux was back in shape. Take a look at Beaux\u2019s story below and follow him on\u00a0    Instagram<\/a>\u00a0to keep up with his new life.

    Beaux became terribly sick and malnourished, and during that time, he went back and forth from local shelters, with rescuers trying to find him a new home, but he was given-up again and again.<\/h3>\n

    Luckily, things changed for Beaux when he met a good-hearted woman named Jamie.<\/h3>\n

    Jamie was truly sent from above, and is here to give Beaux another shot at life.<\/h3>\n

    Jamie knew what it was like to be in foster care because she\u2019s gone through it as a child. So she decided to adopt Beaux, and helped him get back up on his feet again.<\/h3>\n

    \u201cI didn\u2019t care how badly he might be ill,\u201d she told the Dodo. \u201cI just didn\u2019t want him to go into the foster system again and be returned.\u201d<\/h3>\n
