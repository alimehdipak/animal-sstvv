Cats are weird.<\/h2>\n

I don\u2019t mean that in any wrong sense. To me, that is somewhat of a compliment. Cats are mostly famous for their dashing looks and the cute faces they make. Yes, I agree, cats are adorable. However, there is a side of them that only cat owners get to see. You don\u2019t really know a cat until you see it running around the house doing weird shenanigans. We don\u2019t know what is going on in their minds but they are extremely entertaining to look at.<\/p>\n

Even though they may seem like angels from a distance or through computer screens, they are actually devil\u2019s spawns. They wreck your house, cause chaos everywhere and then pretend nothing ever happened. Their weird shenanigans will keep you occupied all day. What kind of \u201cweird\u201d, you ask? Have you ever heard of the term \u201cCats are liquid\u201d? Because it is true. They can take on any shape or form they want.<\/p>\n

30-year-old web\/graphic designer Ainars from Latvia has proven that with his illustrations. His Instagram account,\u00a0<\/span>dailypurrr<\/a>,\u00a0<\/span>is filled with drawings of cats that are sent to him by his fans. Check out some of his hilarious work down below:<\/span><\/p>\n
