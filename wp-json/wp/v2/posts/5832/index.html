As we go through life we go through all kinds of experiences and we learn from those experiences, they help us grow, and we adapt to them. as they say we are a product of our environment. If you think about it every person we meet, every adventure we go on, and every little thing around us is what makes us who we are.<\/p>\n

When a cat and a dog are living together there is a high chance that they will mirror certain patterns and behaviors from one another.
