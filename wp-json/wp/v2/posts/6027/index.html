\nThe animal kingdom never ceases to amaze and inspire, enjoy these wondrous pals that put aside all differences to spread some love. These interspecies friendships show that anybody can get along if they truly put their minds to it. This proves that pets can be much better than humans sometimes:<\/p>\n

they just do not care about differences of size or species; pets will be loyal to their friends for life. If you still believe that they are not capable to form friendships and feel deep emotions, we are sure you will change your understanding before the end of this footage.<\/p>\n

If peoples can determine one thing from pets it should be their ability to see past the exterior and find mutual territory and even harmony in others despite their skin color, their reputation or status, and their origins. Pets are capable of opening their hearts across species boundaries and in effect, unusual and beautiful animal friendships and even a sense of familial affection can emerge.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Have you ever seen a dog and an Emu actually being in each other\u2019s company? Well, if your answer is no, than we happen to be on the same boat. It is more than rare that we see such interesting friendships form. The beautiful thing in animal friendships is that they truly see no difference.<\/p>\n

Sure, they are interested in seeing animals that do not even look remotely as they do, but isn\u2019t that exactly where the magic is. Finding perfection in the imperfection is exactly what life is all about! This video goes long ways to prove just that as these two seem to be hitting it off right off the bat!<\/p>\n
