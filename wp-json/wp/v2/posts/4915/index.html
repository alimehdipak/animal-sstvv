Dogs can do anything.<\/h2>\n

We all understand dogs are very well known for being extremely adorable, and joyous, and they love spending their quality time entertaining their loved ones so all their stress goes away, all their depression and hopelessness go away, ultimately resulting in them living a happy life with their best friend, a perfect example of how companionship works. And the smaller they are in size, the cuter all of this gets.<\/p>\n

But that\u2019s not all they can do. They are great social experts as well. They don\u2019t only look after their loved ones and owners, but also care for society as a whole. When it comes to law enforcement, no one does it better than dogs and we have shared a lot of examples of this in the past as well.<\/p>\n

Today we bring to you another proof of this. Taiwan\u2019s Police K9 unit recently hired Fushin, Schumann, Feida, Yige, AJ, and Liang as the newest members of their force. No, they are not humans, these are little puppies. Once their training is complete, they will be officially hired to run the anti-bomb and anti-drug campaigns. With cuteness this high, everyone is bound to listen to them.<\/p>\n

The parents of this pup gang, Yellow and Leader, are also working as members of the K9 unit. Currently, their point of interest revolves around cuddling, eating, and sleeping but once they are trained well enough, they will become a part of the anti-bomb and anti-drug force and they will also be sent to blood-detection units in Taipei.<\/p>\n
