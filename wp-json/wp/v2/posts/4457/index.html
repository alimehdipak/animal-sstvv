    A video of an adorable dog smiling and wagging her tail after being saved from the streets went viral. The dog\u2019s name is Layla and she was rescued by\u00a0Beaumont Animal Care<\/a>\u00a0after she was seen wandering the streets alone for several days. Rachel Barron, a person of the Animal Care\u2019s staff, visited the dog in her kennel and started talking to her. When she talked in a baby-like voice, the dog was so amused she even smiled. It was probably the first time the little dog got some attention and care from a human. Layla grinned and wagged her pretty, little tail, in sign of appreciation of Rachel\u2019s cute gestures. Luckily, she was able to capture the heartwarming moment in her video camera and it went instantly viral.
    \nNeedless to say, after the video was posted they received a lot of adoption inquiries for Layla, and a local couple was chosen to be Layla\u2019s new family.
    \nOnce they brought her home, Layla quickly got used to her new home and immediately got along with the other dogs of her new family.<\/p>\n
