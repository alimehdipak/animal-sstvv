Cats don\u2019t like staying away from loved ones for too long.<\/h2>\n

Separation is something no pet has worked out yet how to deal with. That\u2019s why you see them all explode with emotions after their owners return to them after a long period of time. They feel loved, but they don\u2019t understand how to contain their happiness, so they just decide to let it all out and that\u2019s why you see them get the most random zoomies, that\u2019s because they really don\u2019t understand how to react. That\u2019s what love does to you and I am happy about it.<\/p>\n
