    When a dog named Monster fell off a shrimp boat, his owner thought she was gone for good \u2014 but days later got an unexpected miracle as the resilient dog made his way home by swimming to shore.<\/p>\n

    Keith \u201cKiwi\u201d Soffes, a shrimp boat captain from San Leon, Texas, loves his dog Monster, who never leaves his side, accompanying him on his daily runs out on the water.<\/p>\n

    But one recent trip turned into heartbreak, after Monster fell overboard. While the dog was no stranger to the water, having accompanied Keith on the boat since she was a puppy, and it was a calm day, the dog was suddenly gone without a trace.<\/p>\n

    <\/p>\n

    Keith searched everywhere, hoping she would turn up, but soon feared for the worst. \u201cI lost the one thing in this world that I truly loved,\u201d Keith\u00a0wrote. \u201cTotally heartbroken.\u201d<\/p>\n

    Keith shared the story on social media, continuing to hope Monster would show up somewhere. He received a lot of support and prayers from people online.<\/p>\n

    But five days after Monster went missing, he got great news: Monster was alive and well\u2026 after having quite an adventure at sea.<\/p>\n

    According to\u00a0FOX 26 Houston, Monster survived by swimming between five and six miles to shore across the Galveston Bay.<\/p>\n

    Monster was found days later after reaching the shore. Keith was stunned after hearing her story.<\/p>\n

    \u201cI said \u2018man, she couldn\u2019t have made that swim,\u201d he told FOX 26. \u201cThere\u2019s no way that dog swam that long.\u201d<\/p>\n

    After many emotional days apart, Keith and Monster were overjoyed to reunite.<\/p>\n

    \u201cI was crying so hard I couldn\u2019t even talk,\u201d Keith said. \u201cShe was giving me so many kisses.<\/p>\n

    Now, Monster is finally safe and sound at home. Keith says that while he\u2019s still exhausted from the emotional search, Monster, despite her long adventure, seems pretty unfazed.<\/p>\n

    \u201cShe acts like nothing happened,\u201d Keith\u00a0wrote on Facebook, along with a photo of the dog happily sleeping. \u201cShe goes to sleep happy as a jaybird \u2026 I think she may have jumped off on purpose. Just to get on TV,\u201d he joked.<\/p>\n
